Testing and Inspection Procedures

Ensuring the quality and reliability of our products is essential. To achieve this, we conduct a series of rigorous tests on our hose assemblies, fittings, and other products.

Pressure Testing

Ensuring hoses withstand designated pressures without leakage or failure.

Burst Pressure Testing

Determining the maximum pressure hoses can handle safely.

Leakage Testing

Guaranteeing leak-free performance under specified conditions.

Dimensional Inspection

Verifying that all products meet precise measurements and specifications.

Chemical Resistance Testing

Testing resistance and compatibility with various chemicals.

Bend Radius Testing

Evaluating the flexibility of hoses to ensure they can be bent to specified radii without compromising their structural integrity.
